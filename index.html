About me

My name is Jim Hoekstra. I like exploring the possibilities of using machine learning and (software) engineering to solve real-world problems. In my current position at Wageningen University & Research I am applying these technologies to the food domain, focusing among other things on research towards improving personalized nutritional advice.

Background

I graduated from the MSc program Artificial Intelligence at the University of Amsterdam. My thesis was on the topic of reinforcement learning applied to multi-agent cooperative environments. The goal was to increase the capability of agents to learn to model and cooperate with other (adapting) agents in the environment.
